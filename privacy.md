# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 29, 2024**

## Basic Math Game Privacy Policy

### Information We Collect

**Authentication Data**
When you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique identifier and, if you choose to share it, your email address. This is used solely to sync your progress and subscription status.

**Usage Data**
We collect anonymous usage data to improve the app, including game scores and progress. This data is not linked to your identity.

**Subscription Data**
Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. We do not have access to your payment information.

### How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the app
- To sync your progress across devices
- To manage your subscription status
- To improve the app experience

### Data Storage

Your game progress is stored locally on your device and, if you sign in, synced through secure cloud services.

### Third-Party Services

- **Apple Sign-In** - For authentication
- **RevenueCat** - For subscription management

### Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what is necessary for app functionality.
